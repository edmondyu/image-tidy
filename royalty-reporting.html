Platform detection is based on the filename containing one of: Google, kobo, koboplus, readmoo, KDP, hyread

Copies rules
Google: Qty or Net Units Sold · Readmoo: 銷售數量 · Kobo: Paid Units · KDP: Net Units Sold · HyRead: 借閱人數 when 備註 contains 單館採購 · KoboPlus: not applicable
Royalty rules
Google (Transaction Report): Payment Amount — already USD, Publisher Revenue % already applied by Google · Kobo / KoboPlus: Estimated Earnings · Readmoo: 總金額 × TWD→USD rate × 0.80 (tax) · HyRead: 權利金 × TWD→USD rate × 0.80 (tax) · KDP: Royalty column
Title / Publisher / Author column aliases
Title ← Title or 書名 · Publisher ← Publisher or 出版社 · Author ← Author, Author Name, or 作者
